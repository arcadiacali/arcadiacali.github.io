Miles & Memories

Here's a simple look at where my passport has taken me by continent, with a few notes about what each place represented taught me. I couldnt represent all the places I've been as that would take months to do, but here are some of my most favorite places!

Where I've Been

A quick snapshot of my most memorable trips.

Country Continent City/Region Year What I learned
Ireland Europe Killkenny, Cork, Dublin Varied Family knows no bounds, even all across the ocean.
Switzerland Europe Shwyz, Zurich 2023 If you plan to go hiking, come prepared with the right gear.
Morocco Africa Marrakesh, Casa Blanca 2023 They are in a water crisis there so depending on the area, water is rationed. Sometimes the water can shut off when showering and drinking water is limited.
England Europe London 2018, 2023 If you love italian food, head to Giovanni's!

Europe

  1. Ireland
    • Dublin — Great for museums
    • Cork — markets and music
    • Kilkenny — Lovely city center
    • Wicklow — Beautiful landscapes and home to Glendalough
  2. France
    • Paris — Incredible art I could spend all day looking at.
  3. Spain
    • Madrid — a place I could call my second home. Incredible people, incredible culture.
    • Toledo — So much history and beautiful architecture
    • Barcelona — Beautiful beachy views and lots of hills
  4. Portugal
    • Lisbon — tiled everywhere and streets lined with trams

Americas

  1. Mexico
    • Playa del carmen — beautiful beaches, lots of resorts
    • Yucatán — home to one of the 7 Wonders: Chichén-Itzá
    • Cozumel — Great place for vacationing and clear waters
  2. Bahamas
    • Nassau — Crystal clear water and good vibes.
  3. Belize
    • Lovely people, great food and weather
  4. United States
    • 14 states — still counting and exploring regions

Africa

  1. Morocco
    • Marrakesh — mint tea and beautiful texiles
    • Casa Blanca – an abundance of cats and a beautiful mosque

Top Culture-Learning Moments

  1. Learning how visas work and how convenient dual citizenship is.
  2. Ordering in a language I was still learning. Kindness goes a long way.
  3. Sharing a table at a market with strangers and trading stories over good meals.
  4. Getting lost and finding the best conversations along the way.
  5. Observing daily routines: commutes, night culture, and shopping sundays.
