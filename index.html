Roaming the world since two months old.

What started as a family trip became a lifelong goal of understanding the world.

Hi, I'm Arcadia! I started traveling at two months old to visit family in Ireland. Eighteen countries, four continents, and fourteen states later, somewhere between airport gates and UNESCO World Heritage sites, I fell in love with culture: how people cook, celebrate, commute, and care for one another.
